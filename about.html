About the Author!

I, Brendan Garrow, am a student at Michigan State University pursueing a bachelor's in Games and Interactive Media. As part of the program, I am taking a web design and development class: MI349. As someone who has worked with some bare bones web development, I thought it would be interesting to see how the professional workflow is and adapt my self-taught style to approach industry standard. This blog itself is a product of my work in this class, as well as my 'newly' refurbished portfolio website, which will be released 'soon'.

Some of my hobbies include:

  • Programming of all kinds
  • Building and repairing computers
  • Playing video games
  • Listening to all kinds of music
  • Gamejams (this is different than programming trust me)

The namesake of this blog has a fun origin: I was raised in Texas, from ages four to sixteen; for all intents and purposes I am a Texan, despite what my Michigan-born mother may have to say. Right before finishing high school I moved back to Michigan, where my new friends gave me the nickname "Texas", and it just kinda stuck. With Texas as a pseudonym, I decided to name my afformentioned portfolio website to include it. I wanted a fun domain name so I sat on ICANN's whois site for half an hour trying to find one that was available. I ended up finding TexasCod.in -- and for a good deal too -- which I fell in love with, buying it up that night. When I needed to come up with a blog name, I took my portfolio's name and rebranded it to fit the new purpose.
